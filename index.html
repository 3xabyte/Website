My name is Matt McBurnie, I am a 3rd year Computer Science student at Wilfrid Laurier University.

I began my coding journey in early 2016 when I was in a Grade 10 Communications Technology class. I was with a small group of friends and we accidentally found the Chrome DevTools. We had a lot of fun messing around with the HTML elements on websites such as Google. Since that was a local change, refreshing would have brought everything back to normal (thankfully.) Later in the course, students had to make a basic website in Adobe Dreamweaver. I thought doing all of this was cool, and decided to take an intro to computer science course where students learned Python. From there I decided to go for a degree in computer science since this was fun!
